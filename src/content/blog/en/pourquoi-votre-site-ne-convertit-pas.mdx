A good looking site that does not convert is still a cost. The problem is rarely the looks. It is the clarity of the path between landing and acting.

## Three common leaks.

- The message talks about you, not the visitor. Start with their problem.
- The action is not obvious. One primary action per page.
- No proof. The visitor has to believe before they act.

## Where to start.

No need to rebuild everything. Rewrite the top of the page around the visitor, make the action single and visible, add concrete proof. Measure, then adjust.

> You do not convert with more visuals. You convert by being understood.
